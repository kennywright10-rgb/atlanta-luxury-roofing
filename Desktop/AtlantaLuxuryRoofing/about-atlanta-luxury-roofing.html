Founded for Luxury

Atlanta's high-end homes demand specialists, not generalists. When we started, general contractors were cutting corners on $1M+ estates—using standard techniques on premium materials like slate, copper, and standing seam metal.

We founded Atlanta Luxury Roofing to serve an underserved market: homeowners with exacting standards and premium budgets. For 18 years, we've built relationships with specialty material suppliers in Vermont, copper fabricators, and review boards across 14 Atlanta jurisdictions.

We only work on materials that matter. The rest of the market can keep commodity projects. We specialize in the homes that require it.

Why Specialty Matters

Material Expertise

Deep Training

Slate isn't installed like asphalt. Copper develops patina in ways that require understanding metallurgy. Standing seam metal has specific expansion dynamics. We train on each material's behavior, not just nailing technique.

Permit & HOA

14 Jurisdictions

Buckhead has different requirements than Johns Creek. Historic districts add complexity. We navigate permit offices, historic preservation boards, and strict HOA review processes—often before the project even starts.

Supply Chain

Direct Relationships

Vermont slate quarries, copper fabricators, DaVinci synthetic slate suppliers—we maintain direct relationships that ensure material quality, custom options, and reliable delivery for premium homes.
