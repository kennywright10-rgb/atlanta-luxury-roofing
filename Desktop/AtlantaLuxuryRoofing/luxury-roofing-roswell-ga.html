How much does luxury roofing cost in Roswell GA?

Luxury roofing in Roswell typically ranges from $18,000 for entry-level standing seam to $200,000+ for natural slate on large estate homes. Most Roswell luxury homeowners invest $45,000–$120,000 depending on material choice, roof size, and complexity.

Most luxury roofing projects in Roswell take 2–6 weeks from start to final inspection, depending on material and roof complexity. Add 1–2 weeks for permit processing. Historic preservation review, if required, can add additional time and is factored into our project timeline estimates.
