Step 1 of 4

Register Your Domain

1

Go to namecheap.com

Search for AtlantaLuxuryRoofing.com — if it's available, buy it. ~$12–15/year. If taken, check: LuxuryRoofingAtlanta.com, EliteRoofingAtlanta.com, or AtlantaArchitecturalRoofing.com.

2

Don't buy any add-ons

Domain privacy is fine ($2–3/year). Skip everything else — you don't need their hosting, email, or SSL. You'll get free SSL from Netlify.

3

Leave DNS settings alone for now

You'll update the nameservers in Step 3 after setting up Netlify. Just buy the domain for now.

Step 2 of 4

Deploy to Netlify (Free Hosting)

1

Create a folder called "atlantaluxuryroofing" on your Desktop

Put all your HTML files inside it. Rename atlanta-luxury-roofing.html to index.html — this is critical, it's what loads when someone visits your domain.

2

Go to netlify.com and create a free account

Sign up with Google or email. The free plan is completely sufficient — it handles unlimited traffic for static HTML sites.

3

Drag the folder into Netlify

In Netlify's dashboard, there's a box that says "Drag and drop your site folder here." Drag your atlantaluxuryroofing folder into it. Your site deploys instantly. You'll get a random URL like sunny-river-abc123.netlify.app — this is your live site before the domain connects.

4

Enable Netlify Forms (so your quote form works)

In your index.html file, find the form tag. Add data-netlify="true" to it. This makes Netlify capture all form submissions and email them to you. Free, no backend needed.

Add This to Your Form Tag

<!-- Find this in index.html -->
<form id="quote-form">

<!-- Change it to this -->
<form id="quote-form" data-netlify="true" name="quote">

Then re-upload (drag the folder again). Netlify will detect the form and start capturing submissions.

Step 3 of 4

Connect Your Domain to Netlify

1

In Netlify: Site Settings → Domain Management → Add Custom Domain

Type in AtlantaLuxuryRoofing.com. Netlify will tell you to update your nameservers.

2

In Namecheap: Update Nameservers

Go to your Namecheap account → Manage Domain → Nameservers. Select "Custom DNS." Replace with Netlify's nameservers (they'll give you 2 values — they look like dns1.p04.nsone.net).

3

Wait 10–30 minutes

DNS propagation takes a few minutes. Netlify automatically provisions your free SSL certificate. Your site will be live at https://atlantaluxuryroofing.com with HTTPS, for free.

Step 4 of 4

Replace the Phone Number

Before the site goes live, replace (404) 632-6599 throughout all HTML files with your real phone number. Use Find & Replace in any text editor (Notepad, TextEdit, VS Code). This needs to happen before you launch ads — every call needs to reach you.

Even better: sign up for CallRail.com ($45/month) and use their tracking number. Every call gets logged, recorded, and tagged with the search term that triggered it. This data becomes your proof deck when pitching contractors. Worth the $45.
