Why Synthetic Slate

All the Prestige.
None of the Compromises.

Natural slate is the gold standard — but at $35–$65 per square foot installed, with 6–10 week lead times and strict structural requirements, it isn't the right fit for every home or every timeline.

Today's premium synthetic slate — particularly DaVinci ReRoofing and CertainTeed NorthGate — replicates the depth, texture, and variation of natural quarried stone with startling accuracy. It's lighter, faster to install, more impact-resistant in Georgia hail events, and carries a 50-year manufacturer warranty. For homeowners who want the aesthetic of slate without doubling their roofing budget or waiting months for stone shipments, it's the intelligent choice.

Why Homeowners Choose It

Built for Atlanta Conditions

Georgia presents a specific challenge: hot humid summers, periodic ice events, and hail seasons that stress any roofing material. Synthetic slate handles all of it.

Class 4
Maximum Impact Resistance

Class 4 is the highest impact rating available from UL 2218 testing — the test that simulates golf ball-sized hail at 90 mph. Most Atlanta neighborhoods near East Cobb and Alpharetta see multi-cell hail every few years. Natural slate can crack; Class 4 synthetic rarely does.

~1 lb
Per Square Foot Weight

Natural slate weighs 8–12 lbs/sqft. At that weight, many Atlanta homes — particularly mid-century and 1970s-80s construction — require structural analysis and sometimes reinforcement before installation. Synthetic slate at 1.0–1.5 lbs/sqft fits on virtually any roof without modification.

50yr
Manufacturer Warranty

Natural stone carries no manufacturer warranty — it's a geological material, not a manufactured product. DaVinci's 50-year limited warranty (lifetime for original owners) covers cracking, peeling, and color fade. For estate planning and property valuation purposes, that documentation matters.

50+
Color & Profile Options

DaVinci offers over 50 standard colors with multi-blend options that replicate the natural color variation of quarried stone. If your HOA requires approval, we can provide full manufacturer sample sets and spec sheets for committee review. Custom color matching is available for historic district submissions.

Our Process

How a Synthetic Slate Installation Works

1
Site Inspection & Structural Review

We inspect the existing roof deck, measure all planes, identify flashing needs, and confirm structural loading capacity. This visit is complimentary and results in your itemized proposal within 48 hours.

2
Permit & HOA Coordination

We pull all required permits — Fulton, Cobb, DeKalb, Cherokee, or City of Atlanta — and manage any HOA or historic district submissions. Typical permit processing runs 1–2 weeks.

3
Tear-Off & Deck Preparation

Full tear-off of existing roofing material, inspection and replacement of damaged decking, installation of synthetic underlayment, and copper flashing at all penetrations. No shortcuts on the substrate.

4
Synthetic Slate Installation

Manufacturer-specified installation by trained crews. DaVinci and CertainTeed require specific fastener patterns, starter courses, and ridge treatments to maintain warranty coverage. Timeline: 1–2 weeks for most Atlanta luxury homes.
