The Strategy

Why Google Ads Before SEO

SEO takes 90–180 days to generate organic rankings. Google Ads generates leads on Day 1. You need leads before you can sign a contractor. You need a contractor before you have revenue. You need revenue to fund growth. Ads are the unlock.

The goal isn't to run ads forever — it's to use ads for 90 days to generate proof, sign your first Atlanta contractor, collect their $3,000/month, and use that cash to fund the next city. Once Atlanta ranks organically (Month 3–4), you redirect that ad spend to City #2. The budget is self-liquidating.

The Math That Makes This Work

Google Ads in Atlanta for luxury roofing will cost approximately $35–$75 per click (high-intent, wealthy homeowner terms). At $75/day = ~1–2 clicks/day. At a 20–30% conversion rate to inquiry, that's 6–9 leads per month from your site form and phone. One closed $100,000 standing seam job at 35% margin = $35,000 gross to your contractor partner. That's nearly a year of territory fees from one job. Your ads pay for themselves the first time a lead closes.

Audience Targeting

In Google Ads, under "Audiences" and "Demographics," apply these filters. Not every impression matters — you want wealthy Atlanta homeowners, not renters or lower-income households.

  • → Household income: Top 30%
  • → Age: 35–65 (peak luxury homeowners)

  • → Location: Atlanta metro (25 mile radius)
  • → Device: All (mobile + desktop)
  • → In-market: Home Improvement
  • → In-market: Roofing Services
Exclude — Remove These
  • ✗ Household income: Lower 50%
  • ✗ Age: Under 25
  • ✗ Renters (if available)
  • ✗ Location: More than 35 miles from Atlanta
  • ✗ Past site visitors who bounced quickly

Conversion Tracking

How to Know If the Ads Are Working

Without conversion tracking, you're flying blind. Set these up before you spend a dollar on ads.

1. CallRail — Phone Call Tracking ($45/month)

Sign up at callrail.com. Get a tracking number that forwards to your real phone. Place this number on the website. Every call is logged, recorded, and shows which keyword triggered it. This is your proof deck when pitching contractors. Critical.

2. Google Ads Conversion — Form Submissions

In Google Ads → Tools → Conversions, add a "Website" conversion for form submissions. After your form submits, it shows a thank-you message — tag that event as a conversion. Google will then optimize your campaigns toward this goal.

3. Google Analytics 4 — Site Behavior

Free. Install the GA4 tag on your site. Link it to Google Ads. This shows which pages visitors view, how long they stay, and where they drop off. Essential for optimizing landing pages over time.

4. Weekly Review Ritual

Every Monday: open Google Ads, check impressions, clicks, conversion rate, and cost per conversion. Pause any keyword with 50+ clicks and zero conversions. Increase budget on keywords with the lowest cost per conversion. This weekly habit compounds significantly over 90 days.

Before You Launch

Complete every item before turning on ads. Running ads to a site without a tracking number or proper landing page wastes money.

  • Domain registered (AtlantaLuxuryRoofing.com) Buy on Namecheap or GoDaddy. ~$12/year. Do this today.
  • Site deployed live on Netlify Create free Netlify account at netlify.com. Upload HTML files. Connect domain via DNS settings. Takes 10 minutes.
  • CallRail tracking number installed Sign up at callrail.com ($45/mo). Get Atlanta number. Replace (404) 555-0190 in HTML with your CallRail number. All calls tracked.
  • Quote form works and sends to your email Use Netlify Forms (free, built in) or Formspree.io ($0 plan) to capture form submissions. Test it yourself before ads go live.
  • Google Ads account created (ads.google.com) Set up at ads.google.com. Billing: credit card. Enable enhanced conversions. Link to Google Analytics 4.
  • Google Business Profile started (business.google.com) Claim "Atlanta Luxury Roofing." Use real Atlanta address. Start verification — takes 5–7 days. Do this in parallel with ads.
  • Campaigns 1, 2, and 3 built in Google Ads Use the keywords, headlines, and descriptions in this guide. Set Campaign 1 budget to $40/day, Campaign 2 to $25/day.
  • Negative keywords added to all campaigns Add all red keywords from the Campaign 3 list above to both Campaign 1 and Campaign 2. This is critical — don't skip it.
  • Spreadsheet to log all leads created Simple Google Sheet: Date, Name, Phone, Address, Material Interest, Project Size Estimate, Source (call/form/search term). This is your proof deck for contractor pitches.
  • List of 20 Atlanta luxury contractors ready Search "standing seam metal roof Atlanta" — contractors on page 2–3 are your buyers. Name, phone, website quality rating. Start outreach after you have 10+ leads logged.
The Pitch

What to Say When You Call Contractors

Call after you've had your first 10–15 leads. You need real data, not a pitch deck. Here is the exact script.

The Opening — Phone Call Script

"Hi, this is Ken. I run a marketing property called Atlanta Luxury Roofing. In the past 30 days I've had [X] homeowners contact us about standing seam and slate projects — most with budgets between $80,000 and $180,000. I'm setting up an exclusive territory arrangement with one contractor per area. Is that something you'd want to hear more about? I can show you the lead log."

If they say yes: Schedule a meeting. Bring a printed CallRail log. Show the call recordings if they ask. Explain the territory model ($2,500–$3,500/month, exclusive, cancel anytime). Offer a 30-day trial at $1,500.

If they say "we do our own marketing": "Completely understand. If that ever changes, I have the infrastructure in place. Who should I follow up with?" Get an email. Move on.

If they ask about your company: "We own the digital assets for the luxury roofing market — the website ranks well for the keywords your customers search. We generate the leads; you close the jobs. Simple division of labor."
