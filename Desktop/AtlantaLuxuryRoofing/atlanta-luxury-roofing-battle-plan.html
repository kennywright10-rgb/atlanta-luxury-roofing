The Thesis

Why This Business Wins

Luxury roofing contractors do $40,000–$180,000 projects and most of them have terrible websites, zero SEO, and no idea what AI search even is. You're not competing with them — you're building the digital infrastructure they don't have, and renting it to them. One $120,000 standing seam job covers 40 months of territory fees.

The model is simple: own the digital demand (rankings, leads, brand authority) for luxury roofing in a market, then rent exclusive access to that demand to the best local contractor. They close jobs. You collect a monthly retainer. As you add cities, the revenue stacks. The business compounds — each city you build makes the next one easier because you have case studies, systems, and proof.

The Critical Insight Most People Miss

Don't wait for SEO to rank before you find your first contractor. Run Google Ads from day one. Generate real leads in Week 2. Walk into contractor meetings with actual proof. SEO takes 90–180 days to rank. Google Ads generates leads on Day 1. Use ads to sell your first contractor, use that revenue to fund more ads and content. SEO is the long-term moat — ads are the weapon to get you there.

Sales Strategy

How to Sign Your First Contractor

Don't sell "SEO" or "a website." Sell leads. Walk in with printed proof of calls and form submissions. The math is too obvious to say no to.

The Opening — Phone or In-Person
"I own a lead generation property called Atlanta Luxury Roofing. In the last 30 days, I've received 23 inquiries from homeowners with budgets of $60,000 or more. I'm looking for one contractor to be the exclusive recipient of those leads in your territory. It's $3,000 a month, cancel anytime. Want to see the inquiry log?"
Why it works: You're not selling a promise — you're selling proof. By the time you have this conversation, you've already run Google Ads for 3–4 weeks and have real lead data to show. The contractor's mental math is instant: one $100K job at 35% margin = $35,000 gross. That's nearly a year of territory fees. The risk is completely asymmetric in their favor.
Handling "How do I know these leads are real?"
"I'll show you the CallRail log — every number, every call recording, every timestamp. I'll give you the first month at $1,500 as a trial. If you don't get at least one serious estimate opportunity in 30 days, I'll refund it. I haven't had to make that refund yet."
Trial close structure: $1,500 trial month removes all objection to getting started. Once they close one $80K job in Month 1, they'll sign a 6-month contract at full rate without hesitation. The trial is the conversion mechanism.

Who to Target First

Look for luxury roofing contractors who have good reviews but a bad website. This is your ideal customer. They do great work, homeowners love them, but they have zero digital presence. Search "standing seam metal roof Atlanta" and "slate roof Atlanta" — find the contractors on Page 2 and 3 of Google. They're doing the work, they just can't be found. That's your customer. They already want leads. They just don't know how to get them digitally.
