Why Cedar Shake

The Warmth No Other Material Can Replicate

Cedar shake is the only roofing material with a genuinely organic character - no two roofs look alike. The natural grain, shadow lines, and subtle color variation create depth that grows more beautiful as the wood silvers over the first few years.

Premium western red cedar contains natural oils that resist moisture, insects, and decay. Properly installed with adequate ventilation and maintained every three to five years, a cedar shake roof in Atlanta can last 40 to 50 years and outlast two or three asphalt replacements in the same timeframe.

We source exclusively from sustainably managed forests in British Columbia, working with premium-grade shakes that have the dimensional consistency and tight grain required for superior performance in Georgia's humid climate.

Our Process

Cedar Shake Installation in Atlanta

Cedar shake installation requires more precision and expertise than most roofing materials. Our process is built around maximizing the roof's lifespan in Georgia's climate.

Step 1

Full Tear-Off and Deck Inspection

We remove all existing roofing material and inspect every square foot of decking for soft spots, rot, or structural issues. Any compromised decking is replaced before installation begins.

Step 2

Skip-Sheathing and Underlayment

We install a skip-sheathing system that creates an air gap beneath the shakes - essential for drying in Atlanta's humid climate. A breathable synthetic underlayment is applied over solid decking areas.

Step 3

Class A Fire Treatment

All shakes receive Class A fire-retardant treatment certified to UL 723 standards - required by most Georgia jurisdictions. We provide documentation for permit filing, HOA approval, and warranty records.

Step 4

Installation and Copper Flashing

Shakes are hand-nailed with stainless steel ring-shank nails at proper exposure. Valleys, chimneys, skylights, and all penetrations are flashed with copper for a watertight, long-lasting perimeter seal.

Common Questions

Cedar Shake Roofing FAQ

How much does cedar shake roofing cost in Atlanta?

+

Cedar shake roofing in Atlanta costs $18 to $35 per square foot installed, depending on shake grade, roof complexity, and fire-retardant treatment. A typical 3,000 sq ft Atlanta luxury home runs $55,000 to $110,000 fully installed. Premium hand-split cedar with Class A fire treatment on a complex roofline is at the top of the range.

How long does cedar shake roofing last in Atlanta?

+

Properly installed and maintained cedar shake lasts 30 to 50 years in Atlanta's climate. The key factors are adequate ventilation beneath the shakes, annual moss and debris treatment, and periodic re-treatment with preservative. Well-maintained shake can exceed 50 years.

Does cedar shake require fire treatment in Georgia?

+

In most Georgia jurisdictions, cedar shake requires Class A or Class B fire-retardant treatment. Cherokee, Fulton, and Forsyth counties typically require Class A fire rating for new installations. We handle all fire treatment as part of our installation process and provide certification documentation for HOA and permit compliance.

How often does cedar shake need maintenance?

+

Cedar shake benefits from inspection and treatment every 3-5 years. This typically includes clearing debris from valleys, treating for moss and algae growth (common in Atlanta's humidity), and applying a penetrating oil preservative. Homes under heavy tree canopy may need more frequent attention.

Can cedar shake be installed on any Atlanta home?

+

Cedar shake works best on homes with a minimum 4:12 roof pitch. It excels architecturally on traditional, craftsman, colonial, and French country homes. HOA approvals are common in Buckhead and Milton for cedar shake, particularly on homes where it matches existing neighboring architecture.
